Abstract

The OTSR ontology provides a formal representation of knowledge related to tourism offerings in various Spanish cities. It includes information on events, attractions, hotel accommodations, restaurants, and transportation systems. It provides a structured framework for representing knowledge about tourist destinations and their associated resources, enabling data interoperability and facilitating advanced personalized recommendation systems.
